Last Updated: March 21, 2025

CTodo does not require users to create an account, and we do not collect personally identifiable information. However, we may collect the following data to enhance your experience:

  • Task Data: Your to-do list items and related details are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

3. Data Storage and Security

  • We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

CTodo does not share your data with third-party services. However, if we use analytics or crash-reporting tools in the future, we will update this section.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • You have full control over your to-do data within the app.
  • If you uninstall the app, all your stored data will be deleted from your device.
  • You can contact us if you have concerns about your privacy.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. Please review this policy periodically.
